This project involves the design and development of a responsive multilevel gaming website prototype known as ArcadePulse.
This website will require to have precisely seven mandatory webpages that include Home, Game Discovery, Player Dashboard, Leaderboard, Seasonal Challenges, FAQ, and Contact.
All pages are supposed to have consistency in navigational design, font style, spacing, and visual aesthetics related to gaming.
Key functionalities will include game challenge cards, leaderboard segments, player achievements, tracking of progress, seasonal events, responsive navigation, and organization of dummy data.
This is an exclusively frontend-based project without any regard for back-end aspects such as multiplayer integration, payment methods, authentication services, database management, and functional games.
